## 3.1. Inference for the Mean, Conditional on the Variance

### 3.1.1. Posterior Inference

Sampling Model: $Y_1, \ldots, Y_n|\theta, \sigma^2 \sim N(\theta, \sigma^2)$  
Prior Distribution: $\theta|\sigma^2 \sim N(\mu_0, \tau_0^2)$

$\begin{aligned}
p(\theta|y_1, \ldots, y_n, \sigma^2) &\propto p(y_1, \ldots, y_n|\theta, \sigma^2) \times p(\theta|\sigma^2) \\\\
&\propto \exp{\left\\{-\dfrac{\sum(y_i-\theta)^2}{2\sigma^2}\right\\}}\times\exp{\left\\{-\dfrac{(\theta-\mu_0)^2}{2\tau_0^2}\right\\}} \\\\
&\propto \exp{\left[-\dfrac{1}{2}\left\\{\left(\dfrac{1}{\tau_0^2}+\dfrac{n}{\sigma^2}\right)\theta^2+2\left(\dfrac{\mu_0}{\tau_0^2}+\dfrac{\sum y_i}{\sigma^2}\right)\theta\right\\}\right]} \\\\
&\propto \exp{\left\\{-\dfrac{1}{2}(a\theta^2+2b\theta)\right\\}} \\\\
&=\exp{\left\\{-\dfrac{1}{2}a\left(\theta^2-\dfrac{2b}{a}\theta+\dfrac{b^2}{a^2}\right)+\dfrac{b^2}{2a}\right\\}} \\\\
&\propto \exp{\left\\{-\dfrac{1}{2}a\left(\theta-\dfrac{b}{a}\right)^2\right\\}} \\\\
&=\exp{\left\\{-\dfrac{1}{2}\left(\dfrac{\theta-b/a}{1/\sqrt{a}}\right)^2\right\\}} \sim N\left(\dfrac{b}{a}, \dfrac{1}{a}\right)=N(\mu_n, \tau_n^2)
\end{aligned}$

### 3.1.2. Parameter Update

$\mu_0\rightarrow\mu_n=\dfrac{\frac{1}{\tau_0^2}\times\mu_0+\frac{n}{\sigma^2}\times\bar{y}}{\frac{1}{\tau_0^2}+\frac{n}{\sigma^2}}=\dfrac{\kappa_0\mu_0+n\bar{y}}{\kappa_0+n}=\dfrac{\kappa_0\mu_0+n\bar{y}}{\kappa_n}$

Posterior Mean $\mu_n$은 Prior Mean과 Sample Mean의 가중 평균이다. 이때 각각의 가중치는 Prior Precision $\dfrac{1}{\tau_0^2}$과 Sampling Precision $\dfrac{n}{\sigma^2}$이 된다. (Inverse Variance를 Precision이라고 한다.) 여기서 Prior Information이 $\kappa_0$개의 Prior Observations에 기반한다고 생각한다면, $\tau_0^2=\dfrac{\sigma^2}{\kappa_0}$라고 나타낼 수 있다. 이를 이용하여 위 식을 변형해주면, $\mu_n$이 각각의 Sample Size를 가중치로 갖는 Prior Mean과 Sample Mean의 가중 평균이라는 보다 직관적인 표현으로 바꾸어줄 수 있다.

$\tau_0^2\rightarrow\tau_n^2=\dfrac{1}{\frac{1}{\tau_0^2}+\frac{n}{\sigma^2}}=\dfrac{\sigma^2}{\kappa_0+n}=\dfrac{\sigma^2}{\kappa_n}$

Posterior Precision은 Prior Precision과 Sampling Precision의 합과 같다.

### 3.1.3. Predictive Distribution

$\tilde{Y}|y_1, \ldots, y_n, \sigma^2 \sim N(\mu_n, \tau_n^2+\sigma^2)$

## 3.2. Inference for the Variance, Conditional on the Mean

### 3.2.1. Several Distributions

**Gamma Distribution**

$X \sim \Gamma(\alpha, \beta) \Rightarrow f_X(x)=\dfrac{\beta^{\alpha}}{\Gamma(\alpha)}x^{\alpha-1}e^{-\beta x}$ ($x>0, \alpha>0, \beta>0$)

**Inverse-Gamma Distribution**

$Y=\dfrac{1}{X} \sim \text{Inv-}\Gamma(\alpha, \beta) \Rightarrow f_Y(y)=\dfrac{\beta^{\alpha}}{\Gamma(\alpha)}\left(\dfrac{1}{y}\right)^{\alpha+1}e^{-\frac{\beta}{y}}$ ($y>0, \alpha>0, \beta>0$)

**Scaled Inverse Chi-Squared Distribution**

$X \sim \text{Inv-}\Gamma\left(\dfrac{\nu}{2}, \dfrac{\nu\tau^2}{2}\right)=\text{scaled Inv-}\chi^2\left(\nu, \tau^2\right)$ ($\nu>0, \tau^2>0$)

### 3.2.2. Posterior Inference

Sampling Model: $Y_1, \ldots, Y_n|\sigma^2 \sim N(\theta, \sigma^2)$  
Prior Distribution: $\sigma^2 \sim \text{Inv-}\Gamma\left(\dfrac{\nu_0}{2}, \dfrac{\nu_0\sigma_0^2}{2}\right)=\text{scaled Inv-}\chi^2\left(\nu_0, \sigma_0^2\right)$

$\begin{aligned}
p(\sigma^2|y_1, \ldots, y_n) &\propto p(y_1, \ldots, y_n|\sigma^2) \times p(\sigma^2) \\\\
&\propto \left(\dfrac{1}{\sigma^2}\right)^{\frac{n}{2}}\exp{\left\\{-\dfrac{\sum(y_i-\theta)^2}{2\sigma^2}\right\\}} \times \left(\dfrac{1}{\sigma^2}\right)^{\frac{\nu_0}{2}+1}\exp{\left\\{-\dfrac{\nu_0\sigma_0^2}{2\sigma^2}\right\\}} \\\\
&=\left(\dfrac{1}{\sigma^2}\right)^{\frac{n+\nu_0}{2}+1}\exp{\left\\{-\dfrac{\sum(y_i-\theta)^2+\nu_0\sigma_0^2}{2\sigma^2}\right\\}} \\\\
&\propto \text{Inv-}\Gamma\left(\dfrac{\nu_0+n}{2}, \dfrac{\sum(y_i-\theta)^2+\nu_0\sigma_0^2}{2}\right) \\\\
&=\text{scaled Inv-}\chi^2\left(\nu_0+n, \dfrac{\nu_0\sigma_0^2+\sum(y_i-\theta)^2}{\nu_0+n}\right)
\end{aligned}$

### 3.2.3. Parameter Update

$\nu_0\rightarrow\nu_n=\nu_0+n$

Posterior Sample Size $\nu_n$은 Prior Sample Size와 Data Sample Size의 합과 같다. Why?

$\sigma_0^2\rightarrow\sigma_n^2=\dfrac{\nu_0\sigma_0^2+\sum(y_i-\theta)^2}{\nu_0+n}$

Posterior Variance $\sigma_n^2$는 Prior Sum of Squares와 Data Sum of Squares의 합을 Prior Sample Size와 Data Sample Size의 합으로 나눈 것과 같다.

## 3.3. Joint Inference for the Mean and Variance

### 3.3.1. Posterior Inference

$\begin{aligned}
p(\theta, \sigma^2|y_1, \ldots, y_n) &\propto p(y_1, \ldots, y_n|\theta, \sigma^2) \times p(\theta, \sigma^2) \\\\
&=p(y_1, \ldots, y_n|\theta, \sigma^2) \times p(\theta|\sigma^2)p(\sigma^2)
\end{aligned}$

Multiplication Rule을 이용하면 Prior Distribution $p(\theta, \sigma^2)$를 $p(\theta|\sigma^2)$와 $p(\sigma^2)$의 곱으로 분해할 수 있다.

Sampling Model: $Y_1, \ldots, Y_n|\theta, \sigma^2 \sim N(\theta, \sigma^2)$  
Prior Distribution: $\theta|\sigma^2 \sim N(\mu_0, \tau_0^2)$, $\sigma^2 \sim \text{Inv-}\Gamma\left(\dfrac{\nu_0}{2}, \dfrac{\nu_0\sigma_0^2}{2}\right)=\text{scaled Inv-}\chi^2\left(\nu_0, \sigma_0^2\right)$

마찬가지로 Multiplication Rule에 따라 Posterior Distribution을 다음과 같이 나타낼 수 있다.

$p(\theta, \sigma^2|y_1, \ldots, y_n, \sigma^2)=p(\theta|\sigma^2, y_1, \ldots, y_n) \times p(\sigma^2|y_1, \ldots, y_n)$

### 3.3.2. Parameter Update

$\sigma_0^2\rightarrow\sigma_n^2=\dfrac{\nu_0\sigma_0^2+\sum(y_i-\theta)^2}{\nu_0+n}$

$\nu_0\rightarrow\nu_n=\nu_0+n$

$\mu_0\rightarrow\mu_n=\dfrac{\kappa_0\mu_0+n\bar{y}}{\kappa_n}$

$\kappa_0\rightarrow\kappa_n=\kappa_0+n$
